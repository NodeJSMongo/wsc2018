You are cordially invited to attend the World Sylhet Convention in Toronto on September 1st and 2nd, 2018.

The Jalalabad Association of Toronto is a not-for-profit organization arranging this convention as a community service for celebration of the cultural heritage of Sylhet. No remuneration is being paid to the participants. We are going to host this two days program in the heart of Bangladeshi community in Toronto at "Grand Palace Convention Center"

Your presence, meeting others and sharing the unique tradition of Sylhet will be highly appreciated by the global Sylheti community at the convention.

Regards,

Rasheda K Chowdhury, Convenor, World Sylhet Convention 2018, Toronto, Canada.

Debobrata Dey Tomal, President, Jalalabad Association of Toronto, Canada.

Mahbub Chowdhury, General Secretary, Jalalabad Association of Toronto, Canada.

Ilias Khan , Press Secretary, Jalalabad Association of Toronto, Canada.
